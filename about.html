About Me

I’m a 23-year-old music enthusiast and current student. I have a bachelor’s degree in digital media arts (with a focus in audio), and I spend a lot of my free time finding and appreciating new music. My favorite genre is Midwest Emo, and that genre makes up a sizeable chunk of my collection. I encourage anyone with an interest in music to indulge in physical media, and hope they can use it to find a deeper appreciation of the medium like myself.
